Ravital Solomon

I work as a cryptographer at NuCypher.

Previously, I was part of the 11th London cohort of Entrepreneur First. I completed my MSc at Oxford in mathematics and theoretical computer science; I wrote my dissertation on lattice-based zero-knowledge proofs, focusing on their application to constructing an anonymous reputation system. My undergrad was in pure mathematics at Berkeley.

I'm also very interested in education. During my time at Berkeley, I worked as a math tutor at the Student Learning Center (SLC). I've also worked at SY Academy (a Berkeley affiliate) as a math teacher/tutor. More recently, I've been volunteering with Minds Matter as a mentor.
